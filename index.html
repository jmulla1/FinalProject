1. L.A Forum













The LA Forum, also known as Kia Forum, is a multi floor indoor arena. This is one of the best known indoor sports venues in the US.

This is the starting point of this virtual tour. The in game name for this location is Maze Bank Arena. From here proceed to Venice Beach by car.



2. Venice Beach













Venice Beach (Vespucci Beach in game) is a popular tourist attraction. Its iconic boardwalk and diverse stores attract people from all over.

From here, move onto Santa Monica Pier which is walking distance.



3. Santa Monica Pier













Santa Monica Pier (Del Perro Pier in game) is known as one of the main attractions Califonia has to offer. The pier along with the Santa Monica beach makes it an absolute must to visit.

Take a ride on the ferris wheel and enjoy the views, then proceed to the Chinese Theater by car.



4. Chinese Theater













The Chinese Theater, which is now known as TCL Chinese Theater, is another must visit spot. Home to red carpet movie premieres, this location is an Califonian icon.

Catch a movie if you'd like, then proceed to the Hollywood Bowl by car.



5. Hollywood Bowl













The Hollywood Bowl (Vinewood Bowl in game) is an amphitheater in the Hollywood Hills. It was named one of the 10 best live music venues in America, and its iconic bowl shape is a must to see

From here you can also see the Hollywood Sign which is next on the list. Proceed by car to the Hollywood Sign.



6. Hollywood Sign













The Hollywood Sign (Vinewood Sign in game) is probably the most iconic sign in all America. This is a definite tourist spot to visit.

Climb up the signs and get a close look at the letters! Then, proceed to the Griffith Observatory.



7. Griffith Observatory













The Griffith Observatory is a must to visit when in California. The views to see from this location are breathtaking, and all of L.A. is able to be seen from here.

After taking in the views, and walking around the Observatory, proceed to the Bixby Creek Bridge by car. The drive will take longer, but is scenic.



8. Bixby Creek Bridge













The Bixby Creek Bridge (Cassidy Creek Bridge in game) is the most photographed bridges in Califonia. Its unique design and location makes it a premiere tourist spot.

Drive along the bride, and pull over to the side to look at the view of the ocean. If you have a parachute why not base jump into the water. Then proceed to Salvation Mountain.



9. Salvation Mountain













Salvation Mountain is an iconic spot all the way in the Califonian desert. This location has numerous murals and areas painted with Bible verses.

This location has been recreated in the game quite well, and definitely a spot to visit.

This marks the end of the trip, proceed to the LA Forum. You will have eventually travelled all around the map, and seen many iconic locations based off real Californian locations!
